Innovations in Literacy

Engineering Literacy for the Next Century

We're pioneers in frontier-discipline EdTech, helping nations and communities embrace online, home-based, and in-person learning models built around AI, computational thinking, and the literacies the next century will demand. We provide the tools and strategy to bring rigorous, standardized learning to the digital landscape, with families and communities included as partners in the process.

The Future of Education

The career expertise the next generation will need requires an entirely new outlook on education. Literacy is no longer just reading and writing -- it's AI fluency, computational thinking, and the languages civilization will run on next. We've invested heavily in literacy programs that treat English and Chinese as the central business languages of the future, with computer programming and AI literacy as core languages alongside them. We teach what the workforce and the frontier will need before the demand curve catches up.

We don't just create the pedagogy and the research -- we build the tools and evidence infrastructure needed to put our methods into practice. Our S.P.A.R.K. framework and Dream Quest learning journey carry a learner from first spark, as early as preschool, through STEAM literacy to career-ready mastery. We also treat education as a lifelong discipline rather than a phase that ends at graduation, building the on-ramp for adults returning to digital learning to stay relevant in a shifting economy.

Enterprise Partnerships

We partner with enterprise education-technology providers to bring frontier-discipline literacy -- AI, computational thinking, the S.P.A.R.K. framework -- to entire school systems and national learning networks, drawing on more than 20 years of pedagogical design experience.

Curiosity Camps Pipeline

Curiosity Camps, job shadowing, and internships bridge the gap between a student's first spark of curiosity and a seat in the industries our own team came from -- AI, aerospace, and Silicon Valley product engineering. DreamTrack instruments engagement across every stage of the Dream Quest journey, producing evidence schools and government partners can audit.

Open-Source LMS

Curiosity Classroom is our open-source LMS, built on the backbone of widely used open-source LMS tools and customized for frontier-discipline instruction. It runs on Moodle and WordPress, so partners with constrained budgets keep full control of their learning ecosystem. See the project on GitHub or contact us for implementation support.

The Full Consortium

The Curiosity Consortium is our full-scale education solution -- spanning Preschool through Higher Education and career placement -- built on frontier-discipline literacy and available as a modular or full-scale partnership, from first spark to career-ready mastery.

We work with governments, school systems, and enterprise partners to translate frontier-discipline literacy into deployable programs, drawing on our original pedagogy, AI-driven Curiosity Coaches, and the auditable outcomes infrastructure behind DreamTrack and Capstone. We assess a partner's existing strengths and gaps, then scope the engagement -- a single Curiosity Cottage, a full K-12 rollout, or anything between -- around what will move outcomes fastest.
