Technical Briefs

Global Literacy

We treat AI, finance, and code as literacies equal to English and Chinese -- the language set the next century's workforce will actually need.

STEAM Education

Our curricula are built by practitioners in AI, aerospace, and physics -- not curriculum vendors repackaging textbooks -- so students train in the same disciplines our own team works in daily.

Family-Centered Learning

Curiosity Cottages and Curiosity Corners bring frontier-discipline learning into the home, giving families a shared space to practice curiosity together.

We design the literacy infrastructure for a workforce that doesn't fully exist yet -- moving learners from first spark to career-ready mastery through literacy programs delivered in centers and online around the world. We can't predict every turn the future takes, but we're confident that AI, aerospace, physics, and frontier-discipline literacy will be central to it. We build the pedagogical methods and evidence infrastructure that prepare learners -- and the civilizations they'll help shape -- for that future. We collaborate with Ministers of Education, world leaders, large education consortiums, and the general public to advance literacy in the core disciplines the next century will run on.
