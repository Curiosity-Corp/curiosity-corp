Decades of
Researching What Makes Minds Curious

Our mission is to make the world more curious. We're a research institute with a pending 501(c)(3) application: we study the psychology of curiosity, design and test the pedagogical methods that spark it, and investigate how learning models — from the traditional classroom to the flip-school — must evolve to keep learners motivated. That research charter extends beyond human learners: we build AI pedagogy for machine and other sentient systems, too. It becomes practice through Curiosity Camps and Cottages, Curiosity Coaches, and the DreamTrack portfolio system — moving every learner from first spark to career-ready, auditable mastery.

Curiosity Chapters

Our regional chapters carry frontier pedagogy — S.P.A.R.K., Dream Quest, and Curiosity Coaches — into local communities. We have opened Curiosity Chapters in Asia, Africa, and Rural America to build the on-ramp to the next century's disciplines wherever a Dreamer is starting from.

Appalachia Region

Mid-Ohio Valley Curiosity

In the Mid-Ohio Valley, we're laying the groundwork for a regional Curiosity Cottage — a physical space engineered to make frontier learning the default. It's the on-the-ground proof point for the AI, physics, and hands-on maker programs we're bringing to this region.

Empowering HubZones

We train HubZone entrepreneurs in the same product-engineering and workforce-pipeline disciplines our own team practices — pairing tried-and-true micro-business fundamentals with a frontier-tech skill set. Appalachia has been a region of continued engagement for over 20 years, where we teach students, coach founders, and build local capacity for the next century's economy.
