Why Get Involved

Your Time or Gift Funds Original Research

We design our own frameworks — S.P.A.R.K. and the Dream Quest journey — rather than license someone else's curriculum. Every path below plugs directly into that work.

Whether you volunteer, donate, advocate, mentor, or found a chapter, you're supporting the same instrumented system: Curiosity Chapters carry it into new regions, and DreamTrack makes the outcomes auditable.

Recurring Ways to Plug In

Not a one-time commitment — these run on an ongoing cadence.

  • Chapter Meetups

    Regional Curiosity Chapters convene local Dreamers on their own cadence — find or start one.

  • Curiosity Camps Cohorts

    Bootcamp-style, project-driven sprints that run in rolling cohorts throughout the year.

  • Curiosity Hive Sessions

    Ongoing community sessions in the Hive, where Dreamers and volunteers work in synthetic agile teams.

  • Curiosity Research Corporation is a 501(c)3 nonprofit designing original curricula and instrumented outcomes for the next century of learners — not licensing someone else's framework.

    Our Mission

  • We exist to push society's literacy to its next frontier — and to build the on-ramp from a student's first spark of curiosity to a seat in the rooms where the future gets built.
