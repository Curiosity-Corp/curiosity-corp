Curiosity Chapters

Build the Local Infrastructure for a Global Mission

A Curiosity Chapter is the regional infrastructure behind the Curiosity Consortium —
local Dreamers convened, Spark Sessions run in person, and a direct line back to the
frameworks and coaches our research team builds centrally.

Convene Local Dreamers

Build a regional cohort of Dreamers — the learners, families, and educators your chapter serves — and connect them to the Dream Quest journey.

Run Spark Sessions Locally

Host in-person Spark Sessions in your region, bringing our microlearning briefings on AI fluency and computational thinking to a local audience.

Plug Into the Consortium

Every chapter connects back to the Curiosity Consortium, the umbrella linking chapters, partners, and programs into one coordinated network.

Recruit Local Coaches

Identify and train local mentors who can work alongside Curiosity Coaches, our AI-driven mentorship program, in your community.

Report Auditable Outcomes

Track your chapter's activity through DreamTrack, the same portfolio infrastructure funders and universities use to verify outcomes.

Represent the Region

Serve as the point of contact for schools, businesses, and government partners in your region who want to work with Curiosity Research Corporation.
