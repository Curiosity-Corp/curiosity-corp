Fundraise with Us

Run Your Own Campaign for the Mission

Fundraising here means organizing a peer-to-peer campaign or an employer-matching
drive on our behalf — not writing a grant. Every dollar you raise routes to a named
program: Curiosity Camps or Curiosity Coaches.

Run a Peer-to-Peer Campaign

Set up your own campaign page and rally your network behind a specific program — Curiosity Camps, Curiosity Coaches, or Spark Sessions.

Launch an Employer-Matching Drive

Pair your gift with your employer's matching program to increase the impact of a single campaign, routed to the program of your choice.

Host a Fundraising Event

Organize an in-person or virtual event — a briefing, a demo night, a dinner — and direct the proceeds to a named Curiosity Research Corporation program.

Fund a Curiosity Cottage or Corner

Rally support behind the physical or home-scale spaces where the Dream Quest journey happens in person.

Track Your Campaign's Impact

Every campaign routes through DreamTrack-linked reporting, so you and your donors can see exactly where the funds went.

Recruit Fellow Fundraisers

Bring other advocates and alumni into your campaign — fundraising compounds fastest as a network, not a solo effort.
