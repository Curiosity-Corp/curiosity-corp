Internships

The on-ramp to the frontier workforce

Curiosity Research Corporation internships put students to work alongside AI researchers, aerospace engineers, and Silicon Valley product veterans on the same curriculum R&D and AI-coaching systems our team ships to Dreamers every day.
Every internship produces portfolio-grade work you can point to in DreamTrack — not busywork, not a certificate of participation.

Real Disciplines

Work directly in AI/ML research, curriculum design, or program engineering — the same disciplines our founding team came from, not a rotation of admin tasks.

A Named Mentor

Every intern is paired with a practitioner mentor for the duration of the internship — a distinct, human relationship, not an AI Curiosity Coach.

Portfolio-Grade Evidence

Finished work is logged in DreamTrack, our auditable evidence system — the kind of record employers and university admissions can actually verify.
