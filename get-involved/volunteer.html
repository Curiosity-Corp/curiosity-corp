Volunteer With Us

Join the Curiosity Hive

Volunteers plug into the Curiosity Hive, the online network where every Dreamer and every volunteer collaborates. Inside the Hive, you'll practice the same S.P.A.R.K. framework and Dream Quest structure our programs run on — Six Sigma, Agile, and roadmapping included — alongside Curiosity Coaches and program staff. Complete the short orientation below to get matched to a role that fits your background, whether that's AI, aerospace, physics, or program operations.
