AI & Emerging Tech

AI fluency and computational thinking, taught as core literacies — not electives — through Spark Sessions and Curiosity Coaches.

Physics & Aerospace

Our team includes physicists and aerospace engineers who bring lab-grade rigor into Curiosity Camps and the Dream Quest journey.

Literacy Redefined

We treat AI, finance, code, and computational language as literacies on par with reading and writing — and instrument the gains through DreamTrack.

Silicon Valley Pipeline

Internships, job-shadowing, and mentorship give Dreamers a direct on-ramp to the companies and labs our own team came from.

The next century will run on AI, emerging technology, physics, and aerospace fluency the way this century ran on reading and writing. We build the original curricula, the AI-driven coaching, and the auditable evidence infrastructure that get a Dreamer there — in partnership with schools, education ministries, and the Curiosity Consortium.
