Be an Advocate

Carry the Mission Into Your Community

Advocacy here isn't a share button — it's a Spark Session you host for your community,
a program brief you hand to a school board, an introduction you make between our
researchers and the people who set curriculum policy.

Host a Spark Session

Bring a Spark Session — our microlearning briefing on AI fluency, computational thinking, and the S.P.A.R.K. framework — to your school, workplace, or community group.

Brief a School or Legislator

Connect our research team directly with a school board, administrator, or legislator evaluating how AI and emerging-technology literacy fit their curriculum.

Share the Program Briefs

Circulate our technical briefs and 5-Year Strategy within your network — the same documents we prepare for grant officers and policy partners.

Champion Literacy Redefined

Make the case, in your own words, that AI fluency and computational thinking belong alongside reading and writing as core literacies.

Introduce a Curiosity Coach

Help a classroom or family discover Curiosity Coaches, our AI-driven mentorship program, as a next step after your introduction.

Join the Consortium Network

Plug into the Curiosity Consortium, the umbrella connecting advocates, chapters, and partner institutions carrying this work forward.
