Careers with Curiosity Research Corporation

Why You Should Join the Mission

We're not a curriculum vendor — we're a research house built by practitioners from AI
and ML labs, aerospace and physics teams, and award-winning Silicon Valley product
groups, engineering the pedagogy of the next century.

Health & Wellness Coverage

Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage for you and your family, because doing rigorous research shouldn't come at the cost of your own well-being.

Remote-First Flexibility

Most roles are remote by design, with flexible hours built for a team spanning AI labs, aerospace shops, and product teams across time zones.

Research-Grade Equipment

The hardware and software you need to work the way our team worked before joining — AI compute, robotics kits, and the tools a research lab expects to have on hand.

Frontier-Discipline Colleagues

Work alongside AI researchers, aerospace engineers, physicists, and Silicon Valley product veterans who treat every program as a testable hypothesis, not a rehashed curriculum.

Conference & Field Travel

Travel to partner schools, Curiosity Camps, and the conferences where the next century's pedagogy gets debated and defended.

Retirement Matching

A retirement plan with employer matching, because building the future shouldn't mean neglecting your own.
