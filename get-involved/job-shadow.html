Job Shadowing

Watch the work before you name the career

A Curiosity Research Corporation job shadow puts you next to a working practitioner — not a brochure, not a job description, the actual desk. You'll see how a curriculum researcher tests a S.P.A.R.K. lesson or how the Curiosity Coaches team debugs an AI mentoring session.
It's a half-day to full-day placement built to make one frontier discipline concrete.

A Real Role, Not a Tour

You sit with one practitioner doing their actual work for the day — not a scripted walkthrough of the building.

Direct Q&A

Ask the person building the method or the model how they actually got into AI, aerospace, physics, or curriculum research.

A Clearer Next Step

Leave with a concrete sense of the discipline — enough to decide whether to pursue an internship or DreamTrack capstone in it.
