K-12 Partnerships

Frontier Pedagogy Inside the Systems You Already Run

Curiosity Research Corporation builds K-12 pedagogical infrastructure designed by AI researchers, physicists, and aerospace engineers — not a vendor repackaging generic edtech. Our tools are built to deploy inside the systems a school already operates, from cloud productivity suites to an existing LMS, without requiring a school to rip out its infrastructure to adopt frontier pedagogy.

Listed below are the programs Curiosity Research Corporation has built specifically for K-12 schools.

Curiosity Classroom

Curiosity Classroom (built under the name Pedadida) is a fully open-source LMS platform designed entirely in-house — not licensed from a publisher. It combines industry-standard, PHP-based open tools (Moodle, WordPress, ResourceSpace, FengOffice, SMF Forum) into a single ecosystem a school can run on its own low-cost servers, with full control of its own data and no dependency on a corporate sponsor's continued goodwill. For schools that want offline-capable access with no internet dependency, or heavy customization control, this is the deployment path we recommend.

Curiosity Hive

Curiosity Hive centers on structured, collaborative learning delivered through whatever cloud productivity stack a school already runs. Inside the Hive, students work in synthetic agile teams — practicing project management, roadmapping, and Agile methodology hands-on rather than reading about it — with content calibrated to whichever standardized assessment track a partner school is preparing students for.

Custom Solutions

Our team is also available for consulting on institution-scale rollouts — from open-source Curiosity Classroom deployments to full pedagogical-system integration. We work with education ministries and school boards to build flipped-classroom concepts, remote-learning tracks, and budget-conscious rollouts of frontier-literacy programs using each partner's existing infrastructure and finances.

Become a Partner

Ready to bring frontier literacy into your classrooms? Talk to our partnership team.

Every K-12 partnership routes into the Curiosity Consortium — the umbrella of programs, spaces, and networks available to a partner school. Consortium programs span Preschool through career advancement, so a partnership can grow with a district's students year over year.

The Consortium connects Curiosity Coaches, Spark Sessions, and Curiosity Camps into one pathway, so a student's progress carries over from program to program instead of resetting each year.
