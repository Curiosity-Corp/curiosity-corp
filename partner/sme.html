Ask About a Partnership

A small-business placement is a single hire; a mid-size enterprise needs a repeatable pipeline. Curiosity Research Corporation partners with SMEs on curriculum sponsorship, staff-facing training, and a structured internship-to-hire track, all built on the same S.P.A.R.K. framework and Dream Quest journey we use across every Curiosity Corp program.

Our Curiosity Classroom LMS is open-source, which means an SME's own learning-and-development team can extend it rather than being locked into a vendor's roadmap.

Sponsorship comes in a few concrete forms: underwriting a cohort of the Curiosity Consortium, funding Curiosity Coaches for your own staff's continuing education, or hosting a DreamTrack-tracked internship cohort sourced directly from our programs.

We also run supplementary learning programs that build lasting curiosity habits rather than one-off engagement, grounded in frontier disciplines — AI, emerging technology, physics — rather than general STEAM content repackaged from existing curricula. These programs are designed to hold a student's attention over years, not a single semester.

Through this approach, our programs have reached students across the communities we serve and helped shift how they relate to learning itself — from something done to them, to something they own. Curiosity Research Corporation designs for smaller student-to-mentor ratios wherever the program structure allows, using digital tools to extend mentor reach without diluting attention. We continue to refine the framework based on what actually improves outcomes for both student and mentor.

Internships, job shadowing, and mentorship placements are core to our pedagogy, not an add-on. We've built partnerships with businesses across sectors and the operational tooling to support them: an onboarding process for placed students, an open-source platform where students can contribute to real projects, digital job-shadowing formats, and DreamTrack instrumentation that gives an SME partner a clear, auditable record of intern engagement and readiness.

SME sponsorships flow directly into the Curiosity Consortium, a full curriculum pipeline spanning preschool through career advancement. Rather than a generic corporate-giving line item, your sponsorship funds a specific, named program your team can point to.

Consortium programs are organized around the S.P.A.R.K. framework and Dream Quest journey, with a consistent focus on frontier-discipline literacy — treating AI, coding, and emerging technology as core languages a student needs to be fluent in, the same way we'd teach reading or a second language. DreamTrack ties every student's progress to auditable evidence, which is what lets an SME evaluate a sponsorship or internship cohort on real outcomes rather than a vague impact narrative. Curiosity Research Corporation continues to bring on business partners at every scale to extend this pipeline, on the belief that a student's potential shouldn't be capped by their zip code.

Global Literacy

A sponsorship funds literacy in AI, finance, and code — the same literacies your own workforce will increasingly need to be fluent in.

Frontier Disciplines

What you're sponsoring is built by practitioners in AI, physics, and aerospace — not general STEM content licensed from a textbook publisher.

Sponsorship You Can Point To

Every sponsorship funds a named program with DreamTrack-evidenced outcomes, not a generic line item.
