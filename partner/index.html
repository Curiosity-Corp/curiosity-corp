Ask About a Partnership

Curiosity Research Corporation partners with institutions — schools, universities, corporations, governments, and intergovernmental bodies — that want frontier pedagogy without building the R&D lab to produce it. Our team of AI researchers, aerospace engineers, physicists, and Silicon Valley product veterans engineers the curriculum, the AI-driven coaching, and the evidence infrastructure; our partners bring the learners, the reach, and the mission. Choose the partnership track built for your institution below.
