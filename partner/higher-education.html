Higher Education Partnerships

Research-Grade Infrastructure for Higher Education

Curiosity Classroom — our open-source LMS platform, originally built under the name Pedadida — gives your institution pedagogical infrastructure designed by the same practitioners who built the S.P.A.R.K. framework and the Dream Quest journey, not a vendor's repackaged course shell. It runs on your own infrastructure, so your institution keeps ownership of its learning data.

Our Curiosity Classroom platform runs on an open-source framework built for the higher-education partners we work with — no proprietary lock-in, no per-seat licensing that scales against you.

Beyond the LMS, we build supplementary programs that keep students engaged between formal coursework — Spark Sessions for microlearning, Curiosity Hive for peer collaboration, and Curiosity Coaches for AI-driven, side-by-side mentorship. Each is built on the same S.P.A.R.K. framework, so a student's progress compounds across programs instead of resetting with each new tool.

Our approach favors flipped-classroom structures and student-leadership roles that lower the effective teacher-to-student ratio, paired with digital tools that give university partners access to an international student body and a wider pool of teaching resources. We continue refining the framework the way a lab refines a protocol — instrumented, tested, and revised, not left static.

Our pedagogical method treats internships, externships, and leadership training as core curriculum, not electives. DreamTrack tracks intern participation with auditable metrics — the same evidence infrastructure a student uses to build their Capstone — so university partners can verify engagement and credit-worthy activity rather than take our word for it.

Become a Partner

Ready to give your students research-grade infrastructure? Talk to our partnership team.

Every higher-education partnership routes into the Curiosity Consortium — the umbrella of programs, spaces, and networks available to a partner institution, from Curiosity Classroom to DreamTrack-verified evidence infrastructure. Consortium programs span Preschool through career advancement, so a partnership can plug in wherever a university's learners actually are.

The Consortium connects Curiosity Coaches, Spark Sessions, and Curiosity Camps into one pathway, so a student's progress carries over from program to program instead of resetting each time.
