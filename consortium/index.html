Engineering Education's Next Chapter

We design the literacy infrastructure for a workforce that doesn't fully exist yet — fluent in AI, emerging technology, physics, and the frontier disciplines the next century will demand. The Curiosity Consortium is the umbrella that unites the member organizations building that infrastructure with us, from entrepreneurship networks to research labs to wellness communities.

More Community Developments

Perez Zeledon Development in Costa Rica

In the transportation hub of Southern Costa Rica, Consortium members have spent years building local connections — through farmers' markets, agriculture, irrigation, dance, design, and English instruction — putting the same practitioner-built pedagogy to work in a very different setting than a maker lab.

View full story

Mid-Ohio Valley: Empowering HubZones

We train HubZone entrepreneurs in the same product-engineering and workforce-pipeline disciplines our own team practices, pairing tried-and-true micro-business fundamentals with a frontier-tech skill set. The Mid-Ohio Valley has been a region of continued Consortium engagement for over 20 years.
