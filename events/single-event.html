Convening

Frontier Pedagogy Briefing

Rolling Sched. Varies Format and duration vary by convening Request Convening Details
  • Donors, Partners & EducatorsAudience
  • Announced Per ConveningLocation

This briefing walks through a specific mechanism — S.P.A.R.K., Dream Quest, or DreamTrack — rather than a general appeal. Expect a working session, not a pitch.

We open with the research question a program was built to answer, walk through the method we designed to answer it, and close with the evidence — DreamTrack records, Capstone artifacts, or cohort outcomes — that lets attendees verify the claim themselves.

Briefings are held for donors, grant and government reviewers, and school, business, and NGO/IGO partners considering the Curiosity Consortium. Dates, formats, and locations are announced per convening — contact us to be notified of the next one, or to request a private briefing for your organization or agency.
