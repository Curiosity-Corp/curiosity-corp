Brand Style Guide

Brand Identity & Design System

The canonical reference for Curiosity Corp brand identity — color, type, logo, voice, and UI components. Every sub-brand and web property shares this foundation.

Section 01

Logo & Wordmark

The Curiosity Corp mark is the organization's registered visual identity. Always use the provided SVG or PNG files — never recreate the logo from scratch. The white version (logo_white.svg) is used on dark or accent backgrounds; the full-color version (logo.svg) is used on light backgrounds.

Default (white) — on dark backgrounds

Full-color — on light backgrounds

Clear space: Maintain a minimum clear space on all sides of the wordmark. Never place the mark on a busy photographic background without an overlay. Never recolor, stretch, skew, or add drop shadows to the mark. Never substitute a hand-drawn or custom SVG — use only the provided files.
Section 02

Brand Voice Rules

Energetic, not hype. We don't overpromise. We communicate with genuine enthusiasm about real learning — the kind that sticks because it started with a real question.

Curiosity-driven. Headlines should spark a question, not answer one. Pull the reader forward.

Wholesome and inclusive. Our Dreamers are kids, teens, and adult learners. All copy must be appropriate for any age. Hive/bee metaphors are welcome — keep them about pollination of ideas and shared discovery.

Bottom-up, never condescending. We believe every person has a dream worth pursuing. Copy never talks down. It invites.

No trademark symbols in digital copy. Write S.P.A.R.K., Dream Quest, Curiosity Hive — without (TM) or (R) glyphs that render poorly across platforms.
