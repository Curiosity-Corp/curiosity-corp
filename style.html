Curiosity Corp Style Guide

The canonical reference for Curiosity Corp brand identity — color, type, logo, voice, and UI components. Every sub-brand and web property shares this foundation.

Section 01

Logo & Wordmark

The Curiosity Corp mark is a teal hexagon with internal lattice lines and a six-ray spark at its center, representing the hive, discovery, and the spark of curiosity. The wordmark pairs it with the name set in a bold, condensed sans-serif.

Clear space: Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" in "Corp" on all sides of the wordmark. Never place the mark on a busy photographic background without a semi-transparent teal overlay. Never recolor the spark rays or hexagon to any color outside the approved palette. Never stretch, skew, or add drop shadows to the mark.

Brand Voice

Energetic, not hype. We don't overpromise. We communicate with genuine enthusiasm about real learning — the kind that sticks because it started with a real question.

Curiosity-driven. Headlines should spark a question, not answer one. Pull the reader forward. "What if homework was replaced by exploration?" beats "We offer project-based learning."

Wholesome and inclusive. Our Dreamers are kids, teens, and adult learners. All copy must be appropriate for any age. Hive/bee metaphors are welcome — keep them about pollination of ideas and shared discovery, never anything suggestive.

Bottom-up, never condescending. We believe every person has a dream worth pursuing. Copy never talks down. It invites.

No trademark symbols in digital copy. Write S.P.A.R.K., Dream Quest, Curiosity Hive — without (TM) or (R) glyphs that render poorly across platforms.

Section 05

Sub-Brand Consistency

Every Curiosity Corp sub-brand inherits this teal/cyan palette and these typographic styles. No sub-brand introduces its own primary color.

Spark Sessions
Microlearning on-ramp. Self-paced, drip-fed sparks with quizzes and points.
Ignite Curiosity
Student-facing front door. Discovery, mid-length content, program directory.
Curiosity Hive
Online hive and community. Swarm agile teams, forums, peer collaboration.
Curiosity Camps
Bootcamp-style immersive programs. Cohorts, sprints, certifications.
Curiosity Coaches
AI-driven agentic guides. 1:1 coaching, adaptive curriculum, mistake-positive.
Curiosity Cottages
Physical curiosity spaces — houses designed for exploration.
Curiosity Corners
Cottage concept scaled to a single room or nook, for home use.
Dream Quest
The full 9-stage learning journey framework. Dream → Capstone.
DreamTrack
Dreamer portfolio and progress tracker. Evidence culture made tangible.
Curiosity Guide / Catalog
Curriculum and subject discovery resources for Dreamers and partners.
Rule: Each sub-brand may use the accent colors (community blue, people purple, physical green) as a secondary color for its category tag or diagram node. The primary UI color on every sub-brand surface is always --teal (#42b8d4).
